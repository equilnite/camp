    <div class="learn">In this activity, you will learn how to transpose data in a song that is stored in a table.</div>

    <p style="clear: right;">
        Recall that <code>play song with pitches and beats</code> expects a table of song data as input, <code>map</code> works through every item in a list, and every item in the table of pitches and beats is one note. So, <code>map</code> applies a function to every note in the <var>Mario Theme</var> and  outputs a table of notes (pairs of pitches and beats).<br />
