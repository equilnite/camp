    <h2>Storing Songs</h2>
    <div class="todo">In the future, there could be an optional exercise at the end of this page to go back and modify their mystery song project to make it tally correct responses and perhaps even deduct points for wrong answers. --MF, 2/27/22</div>
    <div class="learn">In this activity, you will use a variable to store a list of notes.</div>
    <p>
        In the <a href="/student-pages/U1/L2/03-creating-editing-blocks.html?topic=sparks%2F1-functions-data.topic&course=middle-school.html" title="Unit 1 Lab 2: Super Short Stories, Activity 3: Creating and Editing Blocks" target="_blank">Super Short Stories</a> lab, you began using <em>local variables</em> to access the inputs to a block you were editing. Those variables were only available within that block.<br />
        <img class="indent" src="/img/U1/lab02/super-short-story-block-editor.png" alt="Super Short Story, feeling: (feeling) job: (job) action: (action 1) place: (place) action: (action 2) number: (number #) color: (color) plural animal: (animals) food: (food): {report (join words ('The') (feeling) (job) (action 1) ('through') (place) ('and') (action 2) ('over') (number) (color) (animals) ('eating') (food))}" title="Super Short Story, feeling: (feeling) job: (job) action: (action 1) place: (place) action: (action 2) number: (number #) color: (color) plural animal: (animals) food: (food): {report (join words ('The') (feeling) (job) (action 1) ('through') (place) ('and') (action 2) ('over') (number) (color) (animals) ('eating') (food))}" /><br />
        When you need a variable to be available <em>anywhere</em> in your project, you can use a <em>global variable</em>.
    </p>
    <div class="vocabFullWidth">: <strong>Global Variable</strong>
        <p>A <strong>global variable</strong> is a variable that is usable by all scripts in the program.</p>
    </div>
    <p>
        For example, you can use a global variable to store the pitches of a song.<br />
        <img class="indent" src="/img/U2/lab01/set-mary-lamb.png" alt="set ('Mary Had a Little Lamb') to (list (64) (62) (60) (62) (64) (64) (64) (0) (62) (62) (62) (0) (64) (67) (67)))" title="set ('Mary Had a Little Lamb') to (list (64) (62) (60) (62) (64) (64) (64) (0) (62) (62) (62) (0) (64) (67) (67)))" />
        <img class="indent" src="/img/U2/lab01/play-song-mary-lamb.png" alt="play song (Mary Had a Little Lamb)" title="play song (Mary Had a Little Lamb)" />
    </p>
